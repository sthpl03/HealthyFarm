Ingredients & Sourcing

Fresh, Local, and Sustainable Sourced

We are committed to sustainability at every step, from how we source our ingredients to how we serve our food. Our products are locally grown, ensuring that every vegetable and fruit is picked at peak ripeness for maximum flavor and nutrition. We partner with small, organic farms that use sustainable agriculture practices, such as crop rotation, natural composting, and cover cropping to mantain soil health and biodiversity. Our plant-based proteins, including non-GMO soy, lentils, chickpeas and quinoa, come from farms that practice low-impact harvesting to minimize enviromental strain.

  • Sustainable irrigation & Water Conservation

    • We also ensure that our ingredients come from farms that use eco-friendly irrigation systems, such as drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting, to conserve water. Unlike traditional farming, which often leads to runoff and waste, these methods deliver water directly to plant roots, reducing evaporation and ensuring every drop counts. Many of our partner farms also use recycle water systems to further reduce consumption and minimize their impact on the enviroment.

  • Beyond Ingredients: Packaging & Energy Efficiency

    • Sustainability does not stop at our ingredients, it extends to every aspect of our operations. Our packaging is 100% recyclable, eliminating unnecessary plastic waste. In our kitchen we use energy-efficient appliances to reduce water and electricity consumption, cutting down on our overall enviromental footprint. By sourcing locally, we reduce transportation emissions while supporting farmers within our community, ensuring that every dish we serve reflects our values: fresh, ethical, and enviromentally responsible.
